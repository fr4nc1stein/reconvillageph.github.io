About Recon Village

Recon Village is a dedicated space at premier security conferences focused on gathering and analyzing open-source intelligence (OSINT), reconnaissance techniques, and offensive security research. Our village provides:

  • Hands-on workshops on advanced OSINT techniques
  • Live CTF challenges focusing on reconnaissance
  • Tools and methodology demonstrations
  • Lightning talks by industry experts
  • Networking opportunities with fellow researchers

Whether you're a seasoned penetration tester, an OSINT investigator, or a security enthusiast, Recon Village offers valuable insights into the art of digital reconnaissance.
