Submission Guidelines

Talk Formats

Lightning Talk

15-minute quick-fire presentations of tools, techniques, or research findings.

Technical Talk

45-minute deep dives into reconnaissance methodologies and research.

Workshop

2-4 hour hands-on sessions teaching practical OSINT skills.

Tool Demo

30-minute demonstrations of OSINT and recon tools.
